Need to remove a background for a profile photo, product listing, or presentation? You don't need Photoshop. Here's how to do it for free, right in your browser.

## Tips for Best Results

- **High contrast helps** — the clearer the subject vs background, the better the result
- **Good lighting** — evenly lit photos work best
- **PNG output** — always save as PNG to preserve transparency
- **Resolution matters** — start with the highest resolution you have
