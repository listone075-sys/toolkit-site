# How to Convert HEIC to JPG (Free & Online)

Apple introduced HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) with iOS 11 to save storage space on iPhones. It's great for your phone, but not every app or website supports it.

Here's how to convert HEIC to JPG for free — without downloading any software or uploading your photos.

## Why Convert HEIC to JPG?

- **Universal compatibility** — JPG works everywhere: websites, social media, email attachments
- **Easier sharing** — Some apps can't open HEIC files
- **Editing** — Most photo editors work better with JPG
