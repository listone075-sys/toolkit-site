# How to Extract Pages from a PDF Online

You have a 200-page PDF. You only need pages 34–37. Here's how to extract just those pages — no Adobe, no uploads, free.

## Method 1: Extract as Images

Best when you need the pages as separate image files.

1. Go to [PDF to JPG](https://toolcraftbox.com/tools/pdf-to-jpg)
2. Upload your PDF
3. All pages are rendered as high-resolution JPG images
4. Download only the pages you need

## Method 2: Split and Rebuild

Best when you need a new PDF with only selected pages.

1. Use [PDF to JPG](https://toolcraftbox.com/tools/pdf-to-jpg) to extract all pages
2. Select the pages you want to keep
3. Use [JPG to PDF](https://toolcraftbox.com/tools/jpg-to-pdf) to rebuild a new PDF

## Common Scenarios

| Need | Use |
|------|-----|
| Extract one page as image | PDF to JPG |

| Remove blank pages | PDF to JPG → select non-blank → JPG to PDF |
| Extract for presentation | PDF to JPG (download as images) |
| Email attachment too large | Extract only needed pages → smaller file |

## Privacy & Speed

All page extraction happens in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device. This means:
- No file size limits
- Works offline once the page loads
- Instant processing on modern devices
- Sensitive documents stay private
