# How to Merge PDF Files Online (Free, No Sign-Up)

Combining multiple PDF files into one document shouldn't require expensive software or uploading sensitive documents to unknown servers.

## Quick Steps

1. Go to the [Merge PDF tool](/tools/merge-pdf)
2. Drag and drop all your PDF files (or click to browse)
3. Arrange them in the correct order
4. Click "Merge PDFs"
5. Download your combined document

## Why Use Our Tool?

- **Privacy**: All processing happens in your browser. Your PDFs are never uploaded to any server.
- **Speed**: No waiting for uploads — merging happens locally on your device.
- **No limits**: Merge as many PDFs as you want, as often as you need.
- **Free**: No sign-up, no trial period, no credit card.

## Common Use Cases

- Combining scanned pages into one document
- Merging chapters of a report
- Consolidating invoices or receipts
- Assembling portfolio documents
